Key Findings

What the Data Tells Us

🎯

Ad Creative Is Working

2.17% CTR on cold prospecting is well above average for B2B campaigns. The messaging resonates with the target audience and is driving quality traffic at an efficient $3.20 CPC.

🔍

Tracking Gap Identified & Fixed

We discovered and resolved a PostHog tracking issue early on. Corrected data reveals real engagement happening — 21.65% of visitors click through and 7.22% interact with a CTA.

📈

Funnel Optimization Opportunity

The original landing page added an unnecessary decision step. By simplifying the user journey, we expect to see significant improvement in click-through and conversion rates.

⚡

New Landing Page Deployed

A redesigned page now leads with the "One Platform Replaces 4+ Tools" value prop, competitor comparison, and all pricing tiers — capturing interest across every budget level.

Actions Taken

Week 1 Optimizations

Fixed Analytics Tracking

Resolved PostHog integration issue to get full funnel visibility — all events now firing correctly.

Redesigned Landing Page

Consolidated into a single page: value proposition, competitor comparison, all pricing tiers with enterprise highlighted, and social proof from 45,000+ filmmakers.

Optimized CTAs

Hero CTAs now anchor to pricing section instead of booking demos — reducing friction and letting visitors self-select the right tier.

Added Granular Tracking

Every CTA button now has a unique PostHog ID so we can see exactly which elements drive engagement.

Updated Ad Destination

Meta ad URL now points to the new optimized page. No changes to creative or targeting — they're performing well.
